Villager Trading Hall

Description

When I was designing this trading hall, I intended it to be built in the side of a hill. It has a decorated entrance, with a villager face. To enter the trading hall, press the button on the right of the villager face, then run to the left of the face to enter when the pistons retract. Once inside, you will see a bottom floor of villagers. Their workstations can be safely interchanged without the villagers escaping, because there are trapdoors preventing them from walking out. On the balcony, there is ample storage, and a button in the back to exit through a flush piston door and bubble elevator. If you implement this base in your own world, this elevator would lead to the surface (the top of the hill).
