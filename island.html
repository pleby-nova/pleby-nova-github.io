3 Player Floating Island

Description

This 3 Player island base is one-of-a-kind. To get up into it, you need to present your shield at the bottom, where there is a massive piston holding up the island. A calibrated sculk sensor will hear you, and open the 2x2 flush door. Inside, there is storage for mining, a mine entrance, and a bubble elevator up to the main island. Once you get up to the island, you head through a cave. Inside this cave there is a pressure plate that opens a 3x3 spiral door that leads to a storage room for the first player. This player will inhabit the botanical house. It has a lush interior, full of plants and a very open floor plan. It also has a garden outside! The second base is a treehouse. The basement has a storage room and a nether portal. Up in the tree, there are three huts. One has a bed and other decorations. The second one has a bunch of utilities, and the third has an enchanting table. Each hut has a wrap-around porch. The third base is a steampunk style airship. Below deck, there is a bed and other decorations. To get into the blimp, there is a slime block launcher. Inside is a bunch of utilities and storage.
